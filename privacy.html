Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Sunrise Specialty Partners ("we", "us", or "our") operates as a commercial office cleaning marketplace serving the San Francisco Bay Area. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you provide when you use our website or request a quote.

Information We Collect

When you request a quote or download our employee questionnaire, we collect your name, company name, email address, and phone number. We use this information solely to match you with cleaning providers and follow up on your request.

We do not collect payment information, social security numbers, or any sensitive personal data.

How We Use Your Information

We use your contact information to reach out about your quote request, connect you with vetted cleaning providers in our network, and send you relevant follow-up communications. We will never sell your information to third parties or use it for unrelated marketing purposes.

Who We Share It With

To fulfill your quote request, we share your basic contact information with cleaning providers in our network who are submitting proposals for your office. These providers are vetted and agree to use your information only for the purposes of responding to your request.

We use third-party tools including Typeform for intake forms. These services have their own privacy policies and handle your data in accordance with their terms.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to complete your quote request and any follow-up. If you would like your information removed from our records, simply email us and we will delete it promptly.

Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies for analytics purposes to understand how visitors interact with our pages. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.
